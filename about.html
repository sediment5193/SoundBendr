BIO

SoundBendr is a heavy and experimental bass DJ and producer living in Asheville, NC. His heavy hitting sound combines Dubstep and Hybrid Trap into one otherworldly experience. He started throwing together mashups at age 12, then got his first pair of turntables when he turned 13 and taught himself how to mix. He began exploring electronic music production in 2018 and has since been continually growing his craft, drawing inspiration from an endless outside of EDM. SoundBendr’s live sets are where all of these influences truly come alive and are full of high energy mashups, quickly freestyled transitions, and original tracks.


SoundBendr’s debut project, Pantheon EP (2020) is packed with

experimental and psychedelic musical elements interwoven with earth shattering bass.

His collab with Novacc, Have No Fear (2021) took it to an even heavier dimension. This

track is featured on Lift Off, a compilation album with some of the heaviest hitters in

the underground bass scene. Atomic Device (2022) has really cemented the Hybrid Trap sound into his discography and assimilates dark, spacey melodies into grungy bass progressions. Blood Money (2022) is the heaviest track SoundBendr has released to date featuring synthesized electric guitars and neck breaking, tearout inspired basses.
